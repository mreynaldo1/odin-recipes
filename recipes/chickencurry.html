Chicken Curry

Description

A mild delicious creamy chicken curry to warm you up during a cold winters day

Ingrediants

  • chicken breasts
  • Onions
  • Tin of tomatoes
  • Coconut Milk
  • Spices
  • Rice
  1. Dice the chicken breasts into small pieces and fry until golden brown
  2. Add in the chopped onion, gentle fry and add the spices
  3. Add the passata, coconut milk and then gently simmer for 30/40 miutes
