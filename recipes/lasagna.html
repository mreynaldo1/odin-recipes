Lasagna

Description

Delicious and decedant lasagna. Perfect for any time of year. Easy to store, refreeze and reheat. Delicious with a side salad, clowslaw and chips.

Ingrediants

  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Cellery
  • Beef mince
  • Passata
  • 100ml Milk
  • A glass of red wine

Method

  1. Start by making the ragu. Chop 2 sticks of celery, carrots and a onion. Saute with butter and oil until partially cooked. Add the mince and fry until golden brown. Add 100mls of milk and fry off, then add a glass of red and do the same. Add passtta. Cook for 2 hours on a low heat.
  2. Make the beschemel sauce.
  3. Using pasta sheets , combine stories of ragu then beschemel than pasta until the dish is filled. Add pamersan on top
